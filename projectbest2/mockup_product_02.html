Vigolo TSL

The VIGOLO TSL High body mulcher is suited for grass cutting, regrowth, forage, crop, and light timber work between 0-4cm dia. Suited to tractors between 35-120HP.

Standard Fitment:

  • Rotor w/'Y'Blades (3 Blades)
  • PTO Drive Shaft (1"3/8 6 spline)
  • Skids with Lower Plates
  • Rotor w/'Y'Blades (3 Blades)
  • 193mm Rear Roller
  • Mechanical Side Shift
  • Rear Opening Door
  • HD Cast Lug w/25mm Pin

Options On Request:

  • Rear Wheels (in lieu of roller)
  • Hydraulic Side Shift
  • Road Travel Kit
